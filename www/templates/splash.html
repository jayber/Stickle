Welcome to Stickle, the consensual calling app.

Phone calls can be annoying, especially when you're in the middle of something.

And you can never get hold of some people; you just end up in voicemail tennis.

Stickle lets you call and be called when it's convenient for you. It's that simple.

  • Find who you want to Stickle and tap their toggle
  • They'll be notified you want to talk
  • When they're ready, they'll tap "Call now"
  • Your phone will prompt you to call them, just by pressing the green telephone button
